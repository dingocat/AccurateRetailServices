Our Services

Site Surveys

  • Meet with the local authorities to get regional building codes and landlord specifications.
  • We will provide you in the form that you desire, the information you will need to easily design your location

Permitting

  • Work with commercial tenants, business owners, architects, and general contractors in researching the permits required, completing those permits, and doing the follow ups to make sure the permits are acquired.
  • When needed, we can help with a response letter to help clarify the plan review comments from the local governing officials, and for food establishments, the Department of Health.

Project Management

  • Help you manage your project to ensure the standards for construction are met.
  • Communicate with you on the progress and conduct a final walkthrough to ensure it is done the way you intended it to be.
